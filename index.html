How to Play

Guess today's mystery footballer in 8 guesses. After each guess, the cards show how close you are.

🎨 Colours

Green Exact match
Yellow Same group, but not exact
Grey No match

⚽ Clubs

🟢 Green — same club as the target player
🟡 Yellow — different club, but same league
⚫ Grey — different club and different league

If a league is fully accounted for by green clubs, remaining clubs in that league go grey.

🧤 Positions

Positions are grouped: GK · DEF · MID · ATT

🟢 Green — exact position match
🟡 Yellow — same group (e.g. CDM when target is CM), and there are still unmatched positions in that group
⚫ Grey — different group, or all positions in that group already matched

🌍 Nationality

🟢 Green — same country
🟡 Yellow — same continent
⚫ Grey — different continent

📅 Birth Year

🟢 Green — exact year
🟡 Yellow — within 2 years (↑ or ↓ arrow shows direction)
⚫ Grey — more than 2 years off

👟 Foot & Status

🟢 Green — exact match
⚫ Grey — no match
